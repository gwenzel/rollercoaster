    The theming of this ride is its greatest pro and its greatest con. The outside rockwork as well as queue line is beautifully done, giving high expecations for the ride. But the ride proceeds not to live up to this. The rollercoaster itself is comfortable and fun with cool elements. But the videos on the screens do not distract enough from the very obvious empty warehouse you're riding through. Add to that a queue that's always at least an hour long, and becomes a disappointing experience.

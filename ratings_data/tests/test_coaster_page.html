Captain's Review

Voltron is widely praised as a thrilling, high‑intensity coaster that delivers powerful launches, abundant ejector airtime and a long, varied layout. The theming and queue experience are repeatedly highlighted as top‑notch, and the ride’s high capacity keeps wait times reasonable. However, many riders note that vibrations or a rattling sensation can be severe on certain trains and outer seats, making those experiences uncomfortable. Overall, when the train runs smoothly, Voltron is considered one of Europa‑Park’s flagship attractions.
